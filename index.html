Welcome!

Located in Edinburg, Texas, the Business, Education, and Technology Academy is a magnet high school drawing from three counties in the Rio Grande Valley. For short, the school is called South Texas BETA and is known for its variety of career and technical education classes and opportunities offered to its students.


A notable organization on campus is its Technology Student Association chapter. Chapter 513 swas founded by technology teacher Mrs. Vickie Roge and started off with just 20 students; nine years later, we stand with over eighty members and a collection of awards and trophies.
